  • ISHANT SINGH RATHORE

    RATED 5.0 This hidden gem is according to me the optimal pan Asian cuisine serving restaurant in Chandigarh. I used to have a few favourites before I tried hibachi but honestly hibachi surpasses them easily.I was blown away by the quality and flavours of their dishes. I tried the malay laksa curry and cottage cheese and exotic vegetables in hot garlic sauce. Both of them were outstanding. Malay laksa had such a nice distinctive flavour of coconut with mild spice. With this fried rice were served which were good too and complimented the coconut Sauce perfectly. The hot garlic sauce on the other hand was tangy and piquant and with that we ordered chilli garlic noodles which were extremely good too. In dessert we tried the chocolate silk cake which was a bit of a let down but then that was from lush-the bakery and not from hibachi. Do give this outlet a try, its worth visiting if you're pan asian food fan.. (more)
